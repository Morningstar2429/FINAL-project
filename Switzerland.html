Switzerland Tour
Day 1

Paris City Tour

After a continental breakfast, you would set out for an Orientation tour of Paris, see the popular sites such as Arc De Triomphe, Champs Elysees, Grand Palais, Petit Palais, National Assembly, Alexander Bridge, Concorde Square, Magdalene Church, Opera Garnier, Orsay Museum. Later you would visit the Eiffel Tower, the most famous landmark of Paris, and enjoy a panoramic view of the city from its 2nd level. Built in 1889 and designed by the young engineer Gustave Eiffel, the tower stands 324 meters tall. It is one of the most enduring symbols of France and the Industrial Age. Next also enjoy cruising on the river Seine to rediscover the full splendour of the riverbanks and monuments like Notre Dame Cathedral. Later in the evening after Indian Dinner, you could choose an optional sightseeing experience - Paris by Evening (at your own expense).
Day 2

Day at leisure or Full day at Disneyland Paris

After a continental breakfast you could spend your day at leisure Or You could opt for the exciting Optional trip to Disneyland Paris , where you can experience many thrilling rides and fascinating shows. Meet and get clicked with your favourite cartoon characters in a carnival-like atmosphere. Later in the evening get ready to experience the spectacular, not to be missed Illumination & Fireworks of Disneyland Paris. Head to the hotel for a comfortable Overnight stay at the hotel in Paris.
Day 3

Paris to Engelberg

After a continental breakfast at the hotel, you would drive past the famous mustard fields in the French countryside and through Burgundy, famous for its traditional vineyards. Later, enter Switzerland, which is a landlocked country in the cultural and geographical centre of Europe. Renowned for its snowbound Alps, lush green meadows and freshwater lakes, the prosperous country of Switzerland is blessed with a distinct character and dynamism, along with the gorgeous hills, cascading waterfalls, fruit loaded orchards and green blossoming gardens. After an approximate 10hr drive, on arrival, enjoy Indian dinner and then a comfortable overnight stay at the hotel in Engelberg.
Day 4

Jungfraujoch with Interlaken

After continental breakfast, you could spend the day at leisure or opt for an Optional tour to Jungfrau and Interlaken, at an additional cost. Proceed to visit Interlaken, on arrival, you will have some time to take some pictures of this lovely little town which is nestled between two lakes. Next, we proceed to Jungfraujoch, often referred to as the Top of Europe. Jungfraujoch makes for a memorable encounter with snow and ice and has all it takes to become the high point of any tour of Europe. Tourists are often amazed at the scenic splendour they witness from the state-of-the-art Cable Car that takes you up to Eigergletcher station from where you change to the train that takes you to the top. Explore the spectacular sights on offer from the Sphinx Observatory, marvel at the beautiful ice carvings in the Ice Palace & much more. Later proceed for an Indian Dinner and an overnight stay in Engelberg.
Day 5

Day at leisure or Mt.Titlis, Lucerne Rhine Falls - Zurich

After a continental breakfast at the hotel, spend the morning at Leisure Or you could opt to choose and embark on an adventurous journey to Mt. Titlis (Optional), by taking an enthralling 35-minutes long ride to the top, where you switch two different cable cars on the way. The last leg of the ride is on the famous Rotair - the world's first revolving cable car offering beautiful panoramic views of the surrounding glaciers, mountains, and scenery. Atop Mt. Titlis, breath-taking views of the Alps. If weather permits, you could also enjoy a thrilling ride on the famous 'Ice Flyer' along with the Snow Park. Later we all proceed to Lucerne, a beautiful city located at the northern tip of Lake Lucerne. Spend some free time here shopping at the famous 'Bucherer' for Swiss watches, jewellery and souvenirs. Continue the day and visit the majestic Rhine Falls - the largest waterfall in Europe. After an Indian Dinner, check in to the hotel in Zurich.
