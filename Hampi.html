Hampi Educational Tour
Day 1

Mythical Hampi

Walking tour of Hemakuta Hill, Storytelling session on origins of Hampi and founding of Vijayanagara. Sunset viewing at Malyvantha Hill. Live hagaluvesha performance by tribal artisans.
Day 2

SightSeeing of Hampi

Location 1:Temple Architecture :Walking tour of Vittala Temple Complex including Purandara Dasa Mantapa.

Location 2:The Great Scavenger Hunt : Solving puzzles and looking for clues in the former citadel of Vijayanagara. A fun and engaging activity that involves a lot of learning!

Location 3:Vijayanagara Walking Tour : Walking tour of Hampi Vijayanagaras’ Urban core. Looking at town planning, city layout, water management, courtly architecture, social & military life of the medieval city.

Day 3

Historical Visit in Hampi

Location 1:Hampi Prehistory: A field trip that takes us to Neolithic sites with Cave Paintings & petroglyphs that are over 2000 to 3000 years old.

Location 2:Sanganakallu Museum & Site Visit: Visit the Robert Bruce Foote Sangankallu Archaeological Museum for an educational program. Field visit with an expert.
