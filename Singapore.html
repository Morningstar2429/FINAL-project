Singapore Tours
Day 1

Singapore

Singapore city, we see Suntec City, Fountain of Wealth, City Hall, Orchard Road, Swissotel the Stamford – the tallest hotel in Singapore, Parliament House, The Esplanade and take a photo stop at Merlion – Symbol of Singapore, followed by a Bum Boat ride in Singapore River which gives us breath-taking views of contemporary architecture and iconic skylines. In the evening, we take Night Safari to experience a close encounter with the wild animals. We take a Tram ride which takes us through the 7 geographical zones. Later we enjoy dinner amidst the natural environment of Night Safari.
Day 2

Sentosa Island

Today we enjoy a Cable Car ride from Mt. Faber to Sentosa Island. Here we enjoy a ride in the highest open air gondola called the SkyHelix. We also visit Images of Singapore, Madame Tussauds wax museum and 4D Marvel show. Then we visit Observation deck of Marina Bay Sands Sky Park which gives us panoramic views of the Singapore city, followed by a visit to Gardens by the bay – Flower dome - the largest glass greenhouse in the world. Get ready to witness a sound & light show (subject to operation) as the supertree grove lights up.
Day 3

Singapore – Universal Studios – Singapore

Today we visit Universal studios which offers excitement, thrills and unlimited entertainment. Experience thrilling shows and attractions like Light Camera Action, Transformer, Accelerator ride, Revenge of the Mummy, Water world, Jurassic Park, Madagascar - A Crate Adventure, Donkey live, Shrek 4D adventure, Flying Dragon, etc. Later we visit Asia’s biggest SEA Aquarium which houses the rare species of the marine world. Then we take a mono-rail ride. In the evening we get entertained by a spectacular light and water show - 'Wings of Time'. Note: For the adventure lovers those who wants to do more rides, Universal Studios has Fast Pass service available at the entrance at an additional cost.
Day 4

Guided City Tour

Enjoy exploring the wonders of Singapore on this guided city tour that will take you through Merlion, China Town, Little India and more. Start the tour with a quick visit to Suntec City where you can see the Fountain of Wealth. Then, head towards the Padang area, followed by a stop at Merlion Park, and take beautiful pictures with the Singapore icon. Enjoy the vibrant vibe of Chinatown and stop at the Thian Hock Keng Temple. One of Singapore's oldest temples of the Hokkien community. Drive through Little India with the tour ending on a high as you stop at the majestic Faber Peak at Mount Faber, Singapore's iconic hilltop destination Schedule: 09:00 - 12:00
Day 5

Singapore Flyer, MBS SkyPark and Gardens by the Bay

Experience breathtaking, panoramic views on Asia's largest observation wheel. For locals and tourists alike, there is only one place in Singapore that offers a birds eye view of the entire island city from Singapore Flyer. The MBS SkyPark houses a ticketed observation deck, lush gardens, top-notch restaurants and an exclusive, infinity-edge swimming pool with a legendary view. The Sands SkyPark Observation Deck offers you unobstructed 360-degree views of the Singapore skyline.
