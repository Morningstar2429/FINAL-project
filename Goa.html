Athens, Greece Tour

Day 1

North Goa

Spend the day visiting happening markets and beaches in North Goa. Visit beautiful attractions like Fort Aguada, Sinquerim Beach, Calangute Beach, Baga Beach, Anjuna, Vagator Beach and Chapora Fort. Note: Private transfers included. Chapora Fort closes at 5:00 PM, thus, your visit is subject to the availability of time. An extra charge of INR 300 will be applicable between 6:00 PM to 8:00 AM. Dolphin trips and water sports can be availed at your expense between October to May.Get set for a day of adventure in Goa Hop on a shared boat at Sinquerim Jetty and cruise along the waters of the Arabian Sea, taking in the coastal views. Spot the dolphins swimming about in their natural habitat around Coco Beach, Millionaire's Palace, Central Jail and Light House from the boat. Note: The duration of the activity is 1 hour. This activity is operational from 1: AM to 1: PM

Day 2

South Goa

Pristine beaches, Portuguese architecture, shopping and the old world charm - South Goa offers it all Visit Shree Manguesh Temple, Old Goa Churches - Ce Cathedral and Basilica of Bom Jesus. Enjoy shopping at Panjim, Dona Paula (Please note that Taxis are not allowed to go till the jetty and will wait 1km away from the jetty infront of CBI office or designated parking area, client will have to walk for that stretch) & Miramar Beach and relish a Goan lunch at Spice Plantation at your own cost. Please note that Spice Plantation is operational from October to May only. You may spend extra and indulge in Mandovi River Sunset Cruise. In case you opt for Mandovi River Cruise, the tour will end at 8.30 pm.

Day 3

Mandovi River Cruise

Be captivated by a magical sunset as you float over the Mandovi River. Board the cruise from Panjim Police Jetty and enjoy the picturesque views on your 1.5 hour long journey. Relish the delicious snacks on deck and vibe to the DJ's music. You will be served two complimentary drinks and one plate of Chana Aloo Kabli as part of the cruise. Note: The timing of the activity is 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM. Shared transfers from hotels in Arpora, Baga, Calangute, Candolim, Sinquerim, Nerul, Dona Paula, Miramar and Porvorim included.
